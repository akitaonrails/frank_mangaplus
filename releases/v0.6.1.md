## FRANK MANGA+ v0.6.1

Two reader polish touches on top of the v0.6.0 manga-RTL rebuild.

### Page-flip animation on left/right gestures

Vertical scroll keys (Space, ArrowDown, j, PageDown) keep the smooth vertical scroll they had — that muscle memory works.

The **left/right click zones** and **ArrowLeft / ArrowRight** now animate as a manga page-flip instead: the current view slides off to the side, the new view comes in from the opposite side, ~440 ms total. Forward = slides left out, comes in from the right. Back is the mirror.

Two different gestures, two different animations, so what you get matches what you asked for.

### Chapter-change flash

When the visible chapter crosses a boundary mid-read (auto-advance into the next chapter), the chapter name in the header briefly glows with the accent color and a soft halo, then fades back to muted. Easy to miss otherwise — the indicator was changing silently before.

Only fires on real mid-read transitions, not on the initial chapter load.
