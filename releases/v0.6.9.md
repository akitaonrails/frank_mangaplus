## FRANK MANGA+ v0.6.9

Two reader navigation fixes.

### Space bar at the chapter boundary now actually crosses into the new chapter

`advance('forward', 'scroll')` at the end of the loaded scroll called `maybePrefetchNext(true)` to pull the next chapter, then `goToGroupIndex(currentGroup + 1)` to scroll into its first group. The await on the prefetch returned synchronously as soon as the pages were appended, but Svelte's `bind:this={frameEls[gi]}` hadn't run yet — so `frameEls[currentGroup + 1]` was still undefined, and `scrollIntoView` silently no-op'd on a missing element.

Mouse-wheel scrolling worked because the wheel just moves `scrollTop` and doesn't touch `frameEls`. Space, ArrowDown, j, PageDown all sat at the same page while the next chapter was loaded but invisible.

Fix: `await tick()` after the prefetch and before the scroll, so Svelte flushes the new bindings first.

### Click zones use left-based positioning + explicit z-index

Past reports had clicks anywhere on the page seeming to only advance forward, with the "back" zone (right half) not responding. The likely cause was the right zone's `right: 0; width: 50%` getting reinterpreted by the page-frame's flex layout in double-pair mode — the absolute-positioned button can interact oddly with flex contexts on some renderers.

The fix makes both zones left-based (`left: 0` and `left: 50%`), adds an explicit `z-index: 2` so they always sit above the manga page and any flex siblings, and stops click propagation defensively. Should be solid across single and double-pair modes.
