## MANGA Plus Reader v0.1.0

First release.

### What it does

Personal-use desktop reader for [MANGA Plus by Shueisha](https://mangaplus.shueisha.co.jp/). You authenticate once with your existing subscriber `deviceSecret` (extracted from the official Android app) and read on Linux / macOS / Windows.

### Features

- Library, search, title detail with sortable chapter list
- Page-fit reader with snap scroll, click-to-advance, and seamless chapter auto-advance
- Persistent image cache under `~/.cache/mangaplus-reader/`
- Local read-state and "Continue ▶" link per title
- All API calls go through the official `jumpg-api.tokyo-cdn.com` endpoint; nothing self-hosted, nothing scraped
