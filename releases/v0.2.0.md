## FRANK MANGA+ v0.2.0

First public release. Same feature set as the dev preview, with a cleaner setup flow.

### What you get

- Library of your bookmarked titles, with the full English catalog searchable from the same window.
- Title detail with banner art, synopsis, and a virtualized chapter list (One Piece's 1100+ chapters scroll without stuttering).
- A page-fit reader with snap scroll. Click the bottom of the page to advance, top to go back. Or use the keyboard.
- Seamless chapter auto-advance: when you reach the end of a chapter, the next one pre-fetches and appends to the scroll.
- Persistent image cache at `~/.cache/mangaplus-reader/`. Re-opening a chapter is instant after the first load.

### First-time setup

You need an active MANGA Plus subscription and your `deviceSecret` (extracted from a phone or rooted emulator install). Full instructions in [`docs/install.md`](https://github.com/akitaonrails/frank_mangaplus/blob/main/docs/install.md).

The app shows a setup dialog on first launch. Paste the secret in, hit save, you're in.

### Notes

- Windows installer is not code-signed; SmartScreen will warn once.
- macOS bundles are notarized when signing certs are available.
- The Tauri WebView routes image fetches through a custom URI scheme so the cookie that authenticates premium-CDN requests survives the WebView/Rust boundary. Details in [`docs/debugging.md`](https://github.com/akitaonrails/frank_mangaplus/blob/main/docs/debugging.md) if you want to verify the on-the-wire format.
